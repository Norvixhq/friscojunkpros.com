Bulk Pickup Guide

McKinney Bulk Pickup Rules & Junk Removal Guide

McKinney uses an appointment-based system for bulky item pickup — no automatic curbside collection. You get 12 free pickups per year, but there are size limits and specific preparation rules you'll need to follow.

Quick Answer

Available?
Yes — by appointment only
How often?
Up to 12 collections per year at no extra cost
Scheduling required?
Yes — must request before noon the business day before your collection day
Main limit
Roughly 3.5 ft x 3 ft x 9 ft, or two large household pieces per pickup

Accepted Items

Mattresses, furniture, gym equipment, empty water heaters, washers, dryers, refrigerators, freezers, and air conditioning units. Large items that don't fit in the gray trash cart.

What the City Won't Take

Extra trash bags (these aren't considered bulky items), loose materials, and anything that doesn't fit the size guidelines. Small items should be boxed and labeled "Bulky."

Schedule & Timing

You need to submit a pickup request before noon on the business day before your scheduled collection day. McKinney gives each household 12 bulky item collections per year at no additional cost. After the 12th pickup, or for excessive amounts, additional fees apply.

Common Mistakes That Cause Missed Pickups

  • Forgetting to submit the appointment request before the noon deadline
  • Using up all 12 annual collections early in the year
  • Setting out extra trash bags and expecting them to be taken as "bulky" items
  • Leaving loose materials instead of boxing and labeling small items
  • Exceeding the size limit without realizing additional fees kick in
