Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

Frisco Junk Pros ("we," "us," or "our") operates FriscoJunkPros.com as a referral and lead-generation service. We connect homeowners and businesses in Frisco, Texas and surrounding areas with independent, licensed junk removal providers. We do not perform junk removal services directly.

Information We Collect

When you use our website or submit a quote request, we may collect the following information:

  • Contact information: Name, phone number, email address, and pickup address that you provide through our quote request forms or by calling our phone number.
  • Service details: Type of junk to be removed, photos you upload, and any descriptions of the job you provide.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and referring source. This data is collected through Google Analytics and similar tools.
  • Advertising data: If you arrive through a paid advertisement, we may capture the traffic source, campaign, and ad that brought you to our site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Lead matching: Your contact information and service details are shared with one or more independent, licensed junk removal providers in your area so they can contact you with a quote. This is the primary purpose of our service.
  • Communication: We may contact you by phone, text, or email to confirm your request or gather additional details needed to match you with a provider.
  • Service improvement: We use usage and advertising data to improve our website, optimize our marketing, and better serve visitors.

How We Share Your Information

This is important: When you submit a quote request through our website or call our phone number, your information — including your name, phone number, address, and job details — will be shared with one or more independent junk removal providers who may contact you to provide a quote or schedule service.

We do not sell your information to data brokers, marketing lists, or unrelated third parties. Your information is only shared with junk removal service providers for the purpose of fulfilling your request.

We may also share anonymized, aggregated usage data with analytics providers (such as Google Analytics) to understand how our website is used.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies, including:

  • Google Analytics — to understand website traffic and user behavior
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — to measure advertising effectiveness and deliver relevant ads
  • Essential cookies — to ensure basic website functionality

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request that we stop sharing your information with service providers

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@friscojunkpros.com or call (682) 337-2918.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, typically no longer than 24 months after your last interaction with our service.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
